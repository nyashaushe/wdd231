Site Plan - Fridays for Future

Site Name

FridaysForFuture.Earth

This name was selected because it directly represents the global movement while the .Earth domain extension reinforces our environmental focus and global reach. It's memorable, relevant, and aligns with our mission.

Site Purpose

The website serves as a central hub for the Fridays for Future movement, providing:

  • Information about climate change and its impacts
  • Resources for organizing and participating in climate strikes
  • News updates about global climate action
  • Tools for connecting with local chapters
  • Educational materials for climate activism

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"How can I join or start a Fridays for Future chapter in my area?"

Scenario 2:

"When and where is the next global climate strike happening?"

Scenario 3:

"What resources are available to help me educate others about climate change?"

Color Schema

Primary Color (#2E7D32): Used for main headings and navigation elements

Secondary Color (#81C784): Used for accents and highlights

Accent Color (#1B5E20): Used for buttons and call-to-action elements

Typography

Montserrat

Used for headings and navigation items. Bold weight (700) for primary headings, regular weight (400) for secondary headings.

Open Sans

Used for body text and general content. Regular weight (400) for normal text, semibold (600) for emphasis.
